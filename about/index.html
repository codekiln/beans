Beans is a log of coffee, tools, attention, and mornings. It is a command-line metaphor rendered softly in the browser: structured data, quiet observations, and the slow build of a personal knowledge garden.

Each bean is a moment. Most beans hold one cup, sometimes two when the changes are meaningfully related. The interface stays technical and readable, because the details deserve calm light.
