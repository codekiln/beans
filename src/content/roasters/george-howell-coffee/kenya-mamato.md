## farm

George Howell Coffee has returned to Mamuto for years, describing it as one of its
reliable Kenyan anchors: floral, dark-fruited, and sweet. This lot comes from the
Mathagu family's farm on the slopes below Mt. Kenya, where deep red-orange soil
and careful washed processing shape the cup's clarity.

The bag notes violet, cherry, plum, and blackberry. In the cup, that reads as a
gentle but vivid Kenya: floral afterglow, sparkling fruit, and sweetness that
holds together even when the roast is very light.
