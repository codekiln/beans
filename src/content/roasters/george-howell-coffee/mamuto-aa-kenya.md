## farm

George Howell Coffee notes that it has been buying Mamuto since 2006 and describes
the farm as a steady source of ripe, sweet, dark-fruited Kenyan coffee. The farm is
now run by Peter, Jason, and Patrick Mathagu, continuing the work of Walter Mathagu,
whose influence helped put Kirinyaga on the specialty coffee map.

Mamuto sits on the slopes below Mt. Kenya in deep red-orange soil. The family
processes the coffee on the farm through drying, then rests the parchment before
final milling. The result is presented here as a bright, floral, fruit-forward
washed Kenya with blackberry, plum, cherry, and violet notes.
