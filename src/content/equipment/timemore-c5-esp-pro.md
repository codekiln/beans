- Hand grinder with repeatable stepped adjustments.
- Reference point for daily brews and ratio experiments.
- Manual reference: [TIMEMORE C5 ESP Pro Manual Coffee Grinder Instruction Manual on Manuals.plus](https://manuals.plus/asin/B0FHH9GG1M)

## Grind notes

![Comparison chart showing the Timemore C5 ESP Pro numbered stepped dial versus the C3 ESP Pro, including click size and broad brewing ranges](/beans/images/timemore-c5-esp-pro-vs-c3-esp-pro-grind-chart.jpg)

- The manual describes the adjustment dial as `0.015 mm per click`.
- It says turning the top dial clockwise moves finer, and counter-clockwise moves coarser.
- Its starting ranges are broad:
  pour-over at about `1.50-2.00` circles,
  espresso or moka at about `0.50-1.20`,
  and French press at about `2.00-2.40`.
- The manual notes that a small return-to-zero deviation of about `+2 clicks` can be normal.

## Practical notes

- Hopper capacity is listed as `25 g max`.
- For cleaning, the manual recommends dry-brush cleaning only and explicitly says not to wash the grinder with water.
