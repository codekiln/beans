## brew

heated 310.8 g purified water  
preheated [patagonia mug](/beans/equipment/patagonia-sin-represas-mug/) and [hario switch](/beans/equipment/hario-switch/)  
with brown paper [filters](/beans/equipment/hario-v60-03-paper-filters/), as per  
usual. 16.2 g yield, pulled out  
0.2 g for reserves, 16.0 g brewed.  

a bizarre brew. switch down for  
first 80 g, pouring through.   
turned switch up, poured from the mug  
back into switch cone. finished  
248.6 g pour (all the water  
that remained in the pan) by 1:20,  
immersion until 2:45,  
draw down until 4:00.

smiling at the eyes, the patter
