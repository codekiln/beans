## i. before light

kitchen still negotiating with dawn—  
the Maestri timer glows small and insistent,  
counting immersion like a patient metronome.  
two spritzes from the LISAPACK,  
fine mist catching the overhead light,  
then the catchment whispers dry-to-wet.  

## ii. cup

the Patagonia mug took its share of the heat first—  
rim warm, enamel honest—  
so the cup would not arrive shocked.  

## iii. ornament

![Chocolate-toned sparkling tree ornament against a dark ground, glass catching pinpricks of light](/beans/images/2026-04-01-0533-chocolate-foreest-sparkling-tree-adornament.jpg)

a tiny forest hung where the light pools:  
chocolate bark suggestion,  
sugar-glass constellations,  
a dress rehearsal for spring  
in the middle of a stubborn season.  

## iv. listen

kept an album open in the tab bar—  
room-filling, unhurried—  
while the Switch did its slow hinge work.

## v. sip

first taste: bright rim, then cocoa settling—  
the timer had already told the truth;  
the mug kept the middle warm  
long enough to finish the poem.
