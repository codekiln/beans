## grind

heated 316 g purified water  
planning 1.8.0 on the timemore  
coarser ... is that right?  
finca is light-medium roast  
wouldn't that mean finer grind ...

tried 1.8.4 instead
of the 1.8.3 baseline. 

measured 16.1 g finca beans,  
then trimmed 0.1 g to the spares  
for a 16.0 g dose. roast date  
matches the guatemala bag just  
finished: [2026-02-16](/beans/spans/finca-los-angeles-bag-february-2026/).  

3 spritzes [r.d.t.](/beans/terms/rdt/),  
4 shakes.

## taste

tim hecker's *haunt me*  
before first sip, which    
smacked sour, then  
bitter on the right, in  
the back. second shipped  
same valence. when  
holding the liquid   
darkly focused with  
language, gaze  
sizzles on juicy  
horizon:  what does next  
cup need? shorter  
immersion, coarser grind.  

the will to experiment  
comes from the chaotic  
central tendency  
or curse.   
break it  
to rebuild it.  
live with the broken  
to inhabit the  
builder's spirit.
