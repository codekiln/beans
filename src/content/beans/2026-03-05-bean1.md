### brew

preheated with **310.0 g** purified water.

measured **16.1 g** finca beans. used RDT with **3 spritzes** from the aerosolizer and **4 shakes**.

ground at **1.8.0** on the timemore, about **3 clicks coarser** than recent cups; not consciously chosen, just left over from yesterday.

after grind, only **15.8 g** grounds despite no visible clumping or cling.

used **255.7 g** purified water for brew with easy immersion, **2:30** immersion.

accidentally left the pan heated during preheat cycle, so the re-pour was steaming. sleepy.
