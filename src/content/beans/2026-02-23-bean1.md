## Brew Notes

Measured 16.0 g beans and yielded 16.0 g grounds on scale with roughly 0.1 g in grinder catchment. Why? This Maestri house can display tenths of a gram but its sensitivity is likely something like +/- 0.05 g.
Final brew water landed at 255.8 g.
Switch release at 2:34, about four seconds later than planned.
No extra agitation and no structural process changes from yesterday.

## Normal Techniques

- RDT: three spritzes and four shakes of the beans. It's become a standard.  
- Two blooms: 1.) to 200 g, 2.) remainder.
