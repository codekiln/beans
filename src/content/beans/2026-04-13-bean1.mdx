## reflect

![bean log 2026-04-13 mon 0522 page three: felt uncertainty reflection diagram with Pollan, Damasio, Solms, and Friston notes](/beans/images/2026-04-13-mon-0522-felt-uncertainty-p3.jpg)

chapter 2 of michael pollan's 2026  
book about consciousness,  
_[a world appears](https://michaelpollan.com/books/a-world-appears/)_,  
antonio damasio and karl friston were  
mentors to mark solms, who says,  

> consciousness is felt uncertainty.

self-organizing systems,  
like crystals, ant colonies, weather systems,  
ecosystems and humans,  
are selected or evolved towards  
persistence. these consume  
error signals to improve predictions  
about their environmment,  
so as to better survive. 

in a dark forest at night, is that  
black splotch **a bear**?  ... 
_examine with adrenaline_  
no, _just a boulder_.  

as organisms, we evolved to **feel**  
uncertainty, to **sense** it.  

in a moment of decision,  
feeling asks which way to go:  
making a model of the world  
that leads the path that  
decreases uncertainty.
