## reflect

![bean log 2026-04-13 mon 0522 page three: felt uncertainty reflection diagram with Pollan, Damasio, Solms, and Friston notes](/beans/images/2026-04-13-mon-0522-felt-uncertainty-p3.jpg)

chapter 2 of michael pollan's 2026  
book about consciousness,  
_"a world appears"_, turns toward  
antonio damasio, mark solms, and  
karl friston, with solms'  
"consciousness is felt uncertainty"  
set at the center.  

the page maps self-organizing systems,  
prediction, entropy, and survival  
into a little decision-landscape:  
the past behind, the future  
ahead, a shaded danger zone  
falling toward likely death,  
and the living path as the one  
that decreases uncertainty  
about the future.  

in a moment of decision,  
feeling asks which way to go.
