### iv. observe

first sip's sour.  
second sip's flash  
with acid sparkles,  
astonishingly juicy.  
not distasteful, though.  
by the third, peering   
back to that high, rough  
groundscent.  
bitterness shifted  
into chocolate  
sitting mostly along the right edge,  
the tongue's gutterball.
