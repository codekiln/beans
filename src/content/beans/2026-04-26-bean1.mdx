## taste

first sip is multi-  
-dimensional, inter-  
-continental. a flange  
of chocoliberation.  
second chutes fine,  
smooth honey thru  
narrow mental prob-  
-oscus, flip flopping  
bits, pleasure and in-  
-quisition. not sour  
at all but cherry smears  
over the sky of this  
appreciative palette.  

what stages does app-  
-rehension need to be-  
come more real, to be  
realized more deeply?  
experience may not be  
sufficient. sequence  
may be necessary for  
worship, devotion.  

## listen

_nested in tangles_  
by [hannah frances](https://hannahfrancesmusic.com).  

this is fresh chimmi-  
-churri, / a bit drizzly  
popomo / post-postmodern  
swirly cream soda fizzy  
tilting revival dance, not  
yet thwarted in / by retro-  
-grade amnesiata, like joni  
seen waving from the truck  
of a hay rack ride. life's  
contours are bizarre  
flowers, thicker than  
strife, driven together by  
the density of the breeze,  
bobbing heads, nodding  
minds, jagged morning  
breath readies its  
apprehension of the day  
by bootloading a pre-  
-diction market, a par-  
-liamental health  
checksum taken at  
breakfast, on an empty  
heart, with fierce denial  
of mortality.
