## black hole v listening

the silent  
black hole  
period of  
epilogue at  
the end of  
the largest  
stars.  

that, into which  
nothing returns.  
an abyss that has  
ceased communicating  
with the universe  
except through  
what disappears  
at its edge.  

this silence is  
the opposite of  
listening. it lacks  
the active ingredient  
of being-with, of  
anticipation, of  
asking in return.
