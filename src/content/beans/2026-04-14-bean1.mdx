## prepare

finished off the [gallon of purified water](/beans/spans/purified-water-jug-late-march-2026/)  
and opened [another](/beans/spans/purified-water-jug-april-2026/), closing and noting  
the span.  

popping the bag of [mamuto aa](/beans/coffees/george-howell-coffee/mamuto-aa-kenya/), the puff  
of flower, slightly different, deeper,  
the possibility of aging.  

planned, recalibrated **1.9.3** on [timemore](/beans/equipment/timemore-c5-esp-pro/).

## taste

first sip is a sour  
toffee candy.  

second, bitter edge,  
sparklines of acidity  

in the third, a reflection  
of the flower in the bag  
apparates and fades.  

deeper in, the nut  
peels and hides  
a smile of  
pliant moisture.  
in the finish, a gleaming  
violet top, spinning  
on a table above  
a ravine.
