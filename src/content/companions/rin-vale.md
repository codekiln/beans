met forest ranger rin  
years ago while backpacking.    
shared a fire on the trail.  
learned a thing or two  
about their remote brew technique.  
swears by the sawyer filter  
and the mountain water  
for the cleanest tasting coffee.  
closes the eyes while the sun  
licks the pines, calmly witnessing    
the berry notes in the bean.  
never met a better brewer.  

ran into rin several times as  
the years have ticked by.    
introverted, private, but not shy  
about what needs to change in the grind  
to unlock the coffee dimension.
